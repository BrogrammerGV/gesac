What is Global eSports Academy and Clinics?

Welcome to Global eSports Academy and Clinics! Our state-of-the art online educational platform provides quality education and training for the eSports World. Specializing in the development and certification of high school, college, and professional eSports coaches, analysts, directors and competitive programs. GeSAC provides a mobile educational platform to meet the learning needs of individuals wanting to be a part of developing competitive teams around the globe in eSports. As competitive programs begin to take form, we believe there is a need for quality training that is recognized and organized in a way that provides targeted development. Global eSports Academy and Clinics is committed to providing quality online and face to face clinics around the world. Recognized experts in the competitive eSports global community have created insightful instructional clinics to help your specific area of development. The instructional clinic contents have been reviewed for educational quality and approved for the educational platform by the GeSAC faculty. Certifications for key roles involved in eSports competitive programs have been developed and will be awarded to participants completing the required curriculum

GeSAC History

In the fall of 2016, Dr. Jason Bauer and Dr. Jay Prescott, lifelong educators and former coaches, began to see the need for development and education for individuals looking to fill roles within the competitive eSports landscape at the high school, college, and professional levels. As the Directors of eSports at Grand View University they saw firsthand the limited pool of quality applications for positions needing to be filled to run a quality program at the university level. From this need, the formation of the Global eSports Academy and Clinic was put into motion. After a year of research, networking, and creating a state-of-the-art mobile online educational platform, GeSAC was launched! GeSAC is the first to provide quality education and training for the competitive eSports World. Specializing in the development and certification of high school, college, and professional eSports coaches, analysts, directors, casters, creatives, referees and competitive programs. Global eSports Academy and Clinics have worked with recognized experts in the competitive eSports global community to create insightful instructional clinics to help your specific area of development. GeSAC, with the guidance from Drs. Prescott and Bauer, have established a quality review process for all submitted instructional clinic curriculum. Once the material is reviewed and approved for the educational platform individuals can sign up for the courses and be awarded recognized certifications upon completion of the required curriculum. GeSAC also plans to launch live onsite regional and global eSports clinics in 2018. These clinics will host recognized experts in the eSports world as guest clinicians and provide a place for individuals to learn, network, and grow as an eSports professional. Visit the GeSAC events and news page for all the latest announcements about these exciting opportunities
